a few words

Nick Velloff is one of the most diverse and well rounded technologists in today's advertising digital space. As the technical lead and consultant at top agencies such as Deutsch LA, AKQA, R/GA, Sapient, Goodby / Silverstein & Partners, Crispin Porter & Bogusky among others, he lead breakthrough campaigns and platform work for brands such as Volkswagen, Microsoft Xbox, ESPN, Vitaminwater, Sony Playstation, Lexus, Hewlett-Packard, Adobe and Travelers Insurance.

 

Prior to his current role as Chief Product Officer of The Grid, he helped assemble and lead a large team of developers, engineers and creative specialists to launch the award winning VW.com consumer website, and was the lead technologist interfacing with both VW-US and VWAG marketing and IT clients. Nick also led the launch of the award winning Lexus LFA Global Campaign of Toyota’s first supercar.

 

Some of the most interesting and complex campaign and platform work can be credited to Nick. He led the launch of the ESPN TV website, designed to provide viewers with a one-stop online destination for information on what's airing on the various ESPN networks. The site combined all the existing promotional websites for ESPN, ESPN2, ESPN News, ESPN Classic, ESPN Deportes and ABC Sports in a rich, interactive interface.

 

Nick’s work has picked up honors from the Webby Awards for Online Advertising, HP Computer is Personal Again named the prestigious ''US Campaign of the Year'' by Advertising Age magazine, Bright Pink Campaign won "Best User Interface" in the 20th Annual Webby Awards, Best in Show at the MIXX IAB Awards, Nominated for a Cannes Lion, and won a Silver Addy,  as well as a Webby Awards Official Honoree.
